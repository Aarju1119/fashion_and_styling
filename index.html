ABOUT FASHION

Styling in fashion is the art of combining clothes and accessories into outfits representing a well-defined fashion style or combinations of fashion styles with current stylistic trends to confer the wearer with a unique visual identity and personal fashion style.

FASHION TYPES

1. **Classic Elegance**:outfits that never go out of style. 2. **Boho Chic**: attire with an abundance of accessories. 3. **Street Style**: Edgy and casual urban-inspired fashion. 4. **Vintage Revival**:'70s boho, '90s grunge, or '50s pin-up.5. **Minimalist Modern**: Clean lines, neutral colors, and simple, functional designs.6. **Preppy and Polished**: Crisp collared shirts, pleated skirts.7. **Athleisure**: Combining athletic wear with everyday fashion for comfort and style.8. **Gothic and Alternative**: Dark, dramatic looks.9. **Eclectic and Artsy**: Mixing various styles.10. **Romantic and Feminine**:Style of vintage charm.11. **Tomboy and Androgynous**:A unisex look.12. **Casual and Comfy**: Easy-to-wear, relaxed clothing.
